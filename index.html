Our Story

A Café Born
from Passion

Flamingo Cafe Le ɛstaminɛ opened its doors on 29 December 2021 with one dream — to craft a peaceful outdoor space where conversations bloom, flavours surprise, and every guest feels at home. Nestled in the green heart of Sainikpuri, we've grown into one of Hyderabad's most loved neighbourhood cafés.
